# AccuPancakes Morning Grill

> AccuPancakes Morning Grill is a popular destination off of Oak Street in Champaign Illinois that has been serving the community for over 30 years. We offer the best pancakes and coffee for a great price. Our menus include all the standard American breakfast fare you love and the happiest servers in town.

Here are our weekly hours:

- Monday - Friday: 7am - 1pm
- Saturday: 7am - 1:30pm
- Sunday: 7am - 1pm

We are located at 1801 S Oak St on the corner with St Marys Rd in Champaign-Urbana, Illinois. We are a proud landmark on the Central Coast of America!
